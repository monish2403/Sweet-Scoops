The Sweet Scoops Story...

The company was founded in 1972 by Mr. M.C. John, a retired senior bureaucrat in the Kerala government. It is currently led by Mr. Simon John and his team of eminent directors. The company is a pioneer in the ice cream industry in South India. The first factory was started in Kochi, and today, it has over 10 factories and ancillary units spread across South India.

The combined production capacity of the 10 factories ranks among the top 5 in the country. The company also has brand experience centers in Ernakulam, Alappuzha, Kottayam, and Thrissur districts of Kerala, as well as in Mangalore district of Karnataka. Additionally, the company has overseas operations in parts of the Middle East and Africa.
